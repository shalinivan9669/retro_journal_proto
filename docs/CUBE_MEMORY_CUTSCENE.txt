Cube memory cutscene

Press E while aiming at the cube in the main yurt room to start a 13 second fullscreen memory cutscene.

The cutscene uses images from res://assets/textures/cutscene/ and does not change scenes or move the player.
During playback, WASD movement, mouse look, and interaction are locked. The center aim dot is hidden.

After the cutscene ends, player control returns and MemoryPainting becomes visible on the yurt wall.
The cutscene can be replayed by interacting with the cube again. The painting stays visible.
