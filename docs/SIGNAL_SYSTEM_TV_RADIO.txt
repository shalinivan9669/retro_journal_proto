Signal System: TV + Radio

Core node:
- Main.tscn contains SignalStateManager.
- Script: res://scripts/signal_state_manager.gd

Interaction:
- Aim at InteractableTV and press E to cycle TV channels 1..6.
- Aim at InteractableTV and press F to trigger TV evaluation.
- Aim at RadioOnBox and press E to cycle radio frequencies 1..9 and trigger RADIO evaluation.

Special combos:
1. TV 5 + Radio 2 + TV trigger
   Starts the existing CubeMemoryCutsceneUI through the cube_memory_cutscene_ui group.

2. TV 2 + Radio 3 + RADIO trigger
   Activates TemporarySignalDoor in Main.tscn.
   The door leads to res://scenes/levels/InfiniteRoad.tscn.
   The door hides and disables itself before changing scenes.
   Returning to Main.tscn loads it hidden again.
   To use it again, tune TV 2 + Radio 3 and trigger RADIO again.

3. TV 1 + Radio 7 + TV trigger
   Shows the centered signal dialogue window.

4. TV 3 + Radio 5 + TV trigger
   Shows the centered signal dialogue window.

5. TV 4 + Radio 8 + TV trigger
   Shows the centered signal dialogue window.

6. TV 6 + Radio 9 + RADIO trigger
   Shows the centered signal dialogue window.

Where to edit:

  res://scripts/temporary_signal_door.gd
  res://scenes/objects/TemporarySignalDoor.tscn

- Centered half-screen signal window:
  res://scripts/signal_dialogue_window.gd
  res://scenes/ui/SignalDialogueWindow.tscn

- Placeholder radio audio:
  res://assets/audio/radio/
- RadioOnBox has an AudioStreamPlayer3D. Frequency changes play the matching placeholder WAV from that folder.
