Textured Retro Prototype

Current main room:
- Project viewport: 1920 x 1080.
- Shape: clean lowpoly octagonal yurt.
- Main yurt model:
  res://assets/models/yurt/clean_lowpoly_yurt_with_open_shanyrak.glb
- The old generated yurt geometry is disabled as OldBrokenYurt_Disabled.
- The clean yurt has a real open shanyrak at the top.
- SkyDome is visible through the shanyrak.
- The yurt now sits inside a generated steppe environment.
- A physical wall opening exists near the door, so the player can walk outside without changing scenes.
- Size: about 20m across in X/Z.
- Radius: about 10m.
- Walls: generated YurtWall_01 through YurtWall_08.
- Wall height: about 4.6m.
- Roof: 8 sloped segments rising to a large shanyrak opening.
- Shanyrak top height: about 8.6m.
- Sky texture is visible through the opening.
- Player, interactive TV, and door remain human-scale.
- The old interactive cube has been visually replaced by an old TV.
- The old door still starts the InfiniteRoad transition when interacted with.
- Walking around the door through the wall opening leads to the outside steppe.

Where to edit UV scale:
- Open the matching material in res://materials/.
- Change uv1_scale.
- Current suggested values:
  mat_wall.tres: Vector3(3, 3, 1)
  mat_floor.tres: Vector3(4, 4, 1)
  mat_ceiling.tres: Vector3(2, 2, 1)
  mat_cube.tres: Vector3(1, 1, 1)
  mat_door.tres: Vector3(1, 1, 1)
  mat_wall_opposite.tres: Vector3(1, 1, 1)

Where to edit room height:
- Open res://scripts/yurt_room_builder.gd.
- Change wall_height and roof_peak_height.
- The main room geometry is generated by Room in res://scenes/Main.tscn.

Where to edit yurt radius / shanyrak:
- The active yurt is now the CleanYurt GLB instance in res://scenes/Main.tscn.
- The shanyrak opening is part of res://assets/models/yurt/clean_lowpoly_yurt_with_open_shanyrak.glb.
- To change the shanyrak opening size, edit/replace the GLB model.
- Manual wall/floor collisions are under Main/YurtCollision.

Where to edit steppe, pylon, and flowers:
- Open res://scripts/steppe_environment_builder.gd.
- Change pylon_position to move the far power pylon.
- Change flower_density_multiplier to increase or reduce flower/grass density.
- Change positions in _build_steppe_vegetation() to move vegetation patches.

Where to edit cloud speed:
- Open res://scripts/sky_clouds_controller.gd.
- Change cloud_dark_speed and cloud_rose_speed.

Where to edit dialogue background:
- Open res://scenes/ui/DialogueUI.tscn.
- Change the TextureRect texture under Panel.

Controls:
- WASD: walk
- Mouse: look
- E: interact / close dialogue
- F while aiming at the TV: trigger the current TV + Radio signal combo
- Esc: close dialogue / release mouse
- E on the TV: cycles TV channels 1..6.
- E on the radio: cycles radio frequencies 1..9 and triggers RADIO combo evaluation.
- TV 5 + Radio 2 + F on the TV starts the existing CubeMemoryCutsceneUI cutscene.
- TV 2 + Radio 3 + E on the radio shows the temporary door to res://scenes/levels/InfiniteRoad.tscn.
- E on the floor hatch opens res://scenes/levels/UndergroundSteppe.tscn.
- R in InfiniteRoad.tscn: return to the room
- R in UndergroundSteppe.tscn: return to the room

How to verify:
1. Run the project.
2. Confirm Main.tscn opens.
3. Walk with WASD and look with the mouse.
4. Check that walls, floor, ceiling, interactive TV, door, and WallOppositePanel show textures.
5. Look through the shanyrak and confirm the sky dome/clouds are visible.
6. Walk through the opening near the door and confirm the outside steppe has collision.
7. Look toward negative Z from the exit and confirm the power pylon is visible far away.
8. Aim at the TV and press E. The CubeMemoryCutsceneUI cutscene should start.
9. Aim at the door and press E. The InfiniteRoad transition should still work.
10. Press Esc. Dialogue should close or the mouse should be released.
