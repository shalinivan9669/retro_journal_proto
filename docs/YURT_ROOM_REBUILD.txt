Yurt Room Rebuild

Previous room size found:
- Floor mesh was 10m x 10m.
- Wall meshes were 10.2m long, 8m high, and 0.2m thick.
- Ceiling was a flat 10m x 10m box centered around Y = 8.1.
- The room used four rectangular walls: WallNorth, WallSouth, WallEast, WallWest.

Scale change:
- Main room footprint was expanded by about 2x in X/Z.
- The new yurt uses yurt_radius = 10.0, so it is about 20m across.

Current yurt size:
- Radius: 10m.
- Diameter/width: about 20m.
- Vertical wall height: 6m.
- Roof peak / shanyrak height: about 10.3m.
- Shanyrak opening inner radius: 3m, about 6m diameter.

How the 8 walls work:
- The old four-wall rectangular room was replaced by runtime-generated yurt geometry.
- The builder script creates YurtWall_01 through YurtWall_08.
- Each wall is a StaticBody3D with BoxMesh and BoxShape3D collision.
- Wall material: res://materials/mat_wall.tres.

Door:
- Door remains an instance of res://scenes/objects/InactiveDoor.tscn.
- It is placed on the north side of the yurt at about Vector3(0, 1.5, -9.72).
- Door size is human-scale again: about 1.5m wide and 3m high.
- E on the door still transitions to res://scenes/levels/InfiniteRoad.tscn.

Cube:
- Cube remains an instance of res://scenes/objects/InteractableCube.tscn.
- It is inside the yurt near the center at about Vector3(-2.0, 0.5, -1.2).
- It remains 1m x 1m x 1m and interactive.

Shanyrak:
- The roof is built from 8 sloped roof panels.
- The roof leaves a large central opening.
- A visible shanyrak ring is built from 16 small BoxMesh segments.
- 16 radial beams run between the ring and the roof direction.

Sky texture:
- The sky visible through the shanyrak is SkyDisc.
- It uses res://materials/mat_sky_shanyrak.tres.
- That material uses res://assets/textures/fitted/tex_ceiling_red_cyan_noise_fit.png.

Where to change yurt dimensions:
- Open res://scripts/yurt_room_builder.gd.
- yurt_radius controls overall width.
- wall_height controls vertical wall height.
- roof_peak_height controls roof and shanyrak height.
- shanyrak_inner_radius controls the opening diameter.

Where to change materials:
- Wall: res://materials/mat_wall.tres
- Floor: res://materials/mat_floor.tres
- Roof: res://materials/mat_ceiling.tres
- Door: res://materials/mat_door.tres
- SkyDisc: res://materials/mat_sky_shanyrak.tres
